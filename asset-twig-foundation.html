Twig Foundation

A modular building piece created for the current Hues Studios project, designed around clear silhouette, layered texture breakup, and a readable line-stylized finish that translates well in game.

Thoughts

Goals, decisions, and lessons.

The main goal for this piece was to create a foundation element that reads instantly during play while still carrying enough texture personality to support the larger world style. A lot of the work went into balancing clarity with hand-worked surface variation so the asset felt stylized without becoming noisy.

Key Considerations

Silhouette readability, modular connection points, texture hierarchy, and making the surface treatment hold up both in close renders and at gameplay distance.
