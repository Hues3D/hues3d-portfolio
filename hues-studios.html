Game Overview

Stylized survival and base-building with a line-driven visual identity.

Hues Studios is the label for James Hughes' solo game development work. The current project blends stylized 3D assets, readable building pieces, survival systems, and a custom cartoon / line-stylized presentation language.

The goal is to build a cohesive world where asset quality, texturing, placement systems, and ongoing iteration all support a distinctive visual direction rather than generic survival-game presentation.
