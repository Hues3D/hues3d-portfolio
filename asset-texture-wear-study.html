Texture Wear Study

A focused surface study built to test how wear, roughness shifts, and edge treatment can add age without making a material feel noisy.

RoleTexture study and material exploration
SoftwareSubstance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag
FocusWear logic and material storytelling

This study exists to test discipline in texture aging. The best result comes from wear that supports the form and story of the piece rather than becoming decoration on top of it.
