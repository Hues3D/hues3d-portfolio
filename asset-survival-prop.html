Stylized

Survival Prop

A stylized prop focused on strong silhouette, clean readability, and texture choices that support gameplay distance as well as close presentation.

RoleModeling, sculpting, UVs, texturing
SoftwareBlender, ZBrush, Substance Painter
FocusStylized readability and texture hierarchy

The main objective for this asset is to let the form do the first half of the work and the texture carry the second half, rather than depending on texture noise to define the piece.
